# AI Skills and AI CoE Content Package

This folder contains two publication-ready assets built from the AI Skills and AI Center of Excellence plan.

## Recommended Publishing Sequence

1. Publish the short article first:
   - [Claude Skills Are Not Just Prompts. They Are an Operating Layer for AI Teams.](claude-skills-operating-layer-blog.md)
   - Use it as the sharper point-of-view piece for LinkedIn, newsletter, or blog distribution.
   - Primary goal: create urgency around Skills as reusable operating knowledge, not prompt snippets.

2. Publish the long guide second:
   - [The Ultimate Guide to Building AI Skills and an AI Center of Excellence](ai-skills-and-ai-coe-ultimate-guide.md)
   - Use it as the evergreen pillar asset.
   - Primary goal: give founders, startups, and enterprises a complete implementation and governance playbook.

## Positioning

The core thesis:

> The next AI advantage is not better prompting. It is the ability to convert repeatable work into reusable, testable, governed AI capabilities.

The guide and blog deliberately avoid a generic "how to write SKILL.md" treatment. They connect Skills to operating leverage, governance, security, evaluation, and the practical role of an AI Center of Excellence.

## Voice

Use a practical operator voice:

- Experienced, direct, and useful
- No hype
- No vague thought leadership
- No boasting about the author or team
- Clear enough for founders, technical leaders, and enterprise buyers

## Suggested CTAs

- "Turn your best repeatable workflows into governed AI capabilities."

- "Create a Skill registry, risk model, and evaluation loop before AI usage fragments across the company."
